A Skyrim Library

Oliver Norred, 22 December 2020

Finally finished.

The Skyrim Library project has content from every book in Skyrim. I wrote none of it. Riley and I got it all from an ugly1 source and prettified2 it.

Looking back, I should have put a curly-quote–maker in my html puker, which we used. I’m sorry I didn’t do that, Riley.

It’s not completely done until we implement Christmas Mode, but I urge you to check it out. Send me an email if you’d change something—it’s a lot to think about for just Riley and a dud!

  1. The Imperial Library, a community-driven, super extensive site. Lady N, whoever you are, props.
  2. Changed straight quotes to curly, “smart” quotes, made headers big.
