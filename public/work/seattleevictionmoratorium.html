I have a soft spot for projects that are unambitious but turn out useful. So when I was trying to find stuff on the coronavirus eviction moratorium in Seattle and Washington state, and it took me more than ten minutes to find anything of worth, I made a website with a tiny goal: itâ€™s just a table of links.

The first and only page on the website.

It is open source on GitHub, so if you know any useful resources, add them to the table! The data is all in links.csv. The rest of the code is just prettily displaying that table.

I developed a three-color solution that takes inspiration from punk rock and protest music designs, tilting the black backgrounds and setting a drop shadow.

The type treatment is the same as on this website! Degular for the headers and Literata at a small optical size for the body copy.
